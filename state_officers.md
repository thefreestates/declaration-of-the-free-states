  <h1>Signatories — State Officers</h1>
</div>

<hr class="rule">

<p class="lead">
The following elected and appointed officers of the several States are recorded here as having publicly aligned themselves with the principles set forth in the <em>Declaration of the Free States</em>.
</p>

<p>
Inclusion in this register is based upon one or more of the following: a written endorsement issued by the officer or their office, a public statement consistent with the principles of the Declaration, or a verified communication from an official governmental address.
</p>

<p>
Titles and offices are listed for identification purposes only. Inclusion does not imply endorsement of every clause of the Declaration unless explicitly stated.
</p>
